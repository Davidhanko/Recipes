Pizza

Description

This pizza recipe should be home-made, to give it some nice your personal touches.

It is completely up to you which toppings you will use. DISCLAIMER Pinapple doesn not belong on pizza.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cup all purpose flour
  • 100 ml tomato ketchup
  • 1 tomato
  • 2 onion
  • 1 teaspoon chilli flakes
  • 1 teasponn baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 100 gm processed cheese
  • 4 mushrooms
  • 1/2 capsicum
  • 1 teaspoon Oregamo
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella
  • 1 tablespoon dry yeast
  • water as required

STEPS

  1. Prepare the pizza dough.
  2. Prepare the pizza base.
  3. Chop all the vegetables for the pizza.
  4. Spread the sauce and veggies on the base.
  5. Bake the pizza at 250Â°C for 10 minutes.
