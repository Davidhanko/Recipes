Spagheti

Description

Bologne spagheti is one of my most favorite food, if done well.

Therefore, I am showing you this recipe, to make sure, you dont mess up. DISCLAIMER Please do not try to improvise.

INGREDIENTS

  • Worchester sauce
  • Beef bouillon cubes
  • Sugar
  • thyme
  • tomato
  • red wine
  • onion and garlic
  • bay leaves

STEPS

  1. Saute garlic and onion
  2. brown the beef
  3. add everything else
