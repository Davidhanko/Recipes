Chicken soup

Description

This chicken soup has amazing healing properties, anyone who drinks this soup will feel much better.

It also disables pain, making it really good for adrenaline activities. DISCLAIMER For anyones harm done after drinking this soup is not mine

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 2,8 liters of homemade chicken stock
  • 6 medium cloves garlic
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 large carrot
  • 1 medium turnip
  • 1 medium yellow onion
  • 1 large parsnip
  • 2 medium ribs celery
  • Kosher salt
  • Minced fresh dill

STEPS

  1. Cut chicken breasts
  2. Heat stock over medium-high heat until tempeture reaches between 66°C and 71°C
  3. Bring soup to a simmer and continue cooking for an hour and prepare other ingredients
  4. Put all other resources into the cooking pot
